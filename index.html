Literature types



The main types of literature are:


1. Fiction: Imaginary stories (e.g., novels, short stories).

2. Non-Fiction: Factual works (e.g., biographies, essays).

3. Poetry: Expressive and rhythmic writing (e.g., sonnets, haikus).

4. Drama: Literature for performance (e.g., plays, scripts).

5. Prose: Ordinary written language (e.g., articles, essays).

6. Folklore: Traditional stories and myths.

7. Epics: Long narrative tales of heroism.
